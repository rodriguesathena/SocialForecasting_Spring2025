Question 6

The Drift Method is best for forecasting the Number of Afghan Refugees coming into Ireland. The Average and Naive methods show no change in the number of refugees over time, which is known to be false when compared to the actual data. To improve the drift method, time series cross-validation could be added to assess the models predictive accuracy over time.
